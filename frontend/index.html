Process

How It Works

// 01
🔐 Authenticate
Verify your identity using your government-issued voter ID and OTP. Your credentials are hashed locally — we never store plain passwords.
// 02
🗳️ Cast Your Vote
Select your candidate from the ballot. Your choice is encrypted client-side using your unique session key before transmission.
// 03
⛓️ Blockchain Record
Your encrypted vote is sent to the smart contract. The transaction is mined and permanently stored on-chain.
// 04
✅ Verify Anytime
Use your transaction hash to independently verify your vote was counted correctly. Anyone can audit results in real time.
Security

Built for Trust

🔒
End-to-End Encryption
Every vote is encrypted using AES-256 before leaving your browser. Not even election administrators can see individual choices.
👁️
Public Auditability
All transactions are publicly visible on the blockchain. Independent auditors can verify results without compromising voter privacy.
🚫
Double-Vote Prevention
Smart contract logic enforces one-vote-per-wallet rules. Attempting to vote twice will be automatically rejected by the network.
🧾
Immutable Records
Once recorded on the blockchain, votes cannot be altered, deleted, or manipulated by any party — including platform operators.
🌐
Decentralized
No single point of failure. The voting contract runs across thousands of nodes worldwide, ensuring 99.9%+ availability.
🪙
ZK-Proof Anonymity
Zero-knowledge proofs ensure votes are valid without revealing voter identity. Privacy and verifiability without contradiction.
